Revolutionizing an Industry

Freedom of Choice and Freedom of Information are Revre's Guiding Principles.

Power to the People

We aim to make it easy for you to complete transactions in real estate. Whether you are selling your property, buying your dream home, or a landlord looking to better manage your rentals, REVRE is focused on helping you to make it seamless, cheap, and fast. We believe in fully open information for the public, letting you see sold data, the most up to date listings, and education so that you can truly do it yourself. If you aren't comfortable going it alone, we have a network of agents that can help.

Rethink Your Notion of Real Estate

Since the beginning of time, buying and selling real estate has been done the same way. Why should you put your trust in someone else? We are creating the future of transactional trust and efficiency.
